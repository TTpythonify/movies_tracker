1. Follow / Social Features

Users can follow other users.

See a feed of what movies your friends added/reviewed.

Adds relational behavior between users.




2. Likes / Upvotes on Reviews

Users can like or upvote reviews.

Sort reviews by most liked for each movie.

Encourages engagement and adds dynamic queries in MongoDB.





3. Search Enhancements

Autocomplete search bar with movie suggestions (JS + API).

Filter movies by genre, year, rating.

Use cached API data for faster results.





4. Activity Feed / Notifications

Expand notifications to real-time feed of activity (WebSockets optional).

Show who added or reviewed a movie recently.






5. User Profiles

Users can have profiles showing:

Their lists (watch later, watched, favorites)

Their reviews and ratings

Average rating given across all movies
